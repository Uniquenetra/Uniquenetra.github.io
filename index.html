My Data Science Story

My journey into data science started with a 'baby' project as a Project Manager for a demand forecasting project for one of the largest retailers in Thailand. The algorithms involved and the entire process piqued my interest in data science. Even after implementing this as a business analyst and project manager, I decided to dive deeper into machine learning and technology. To pursue this passion, I enrolled in one of the top universities in Portugal, embracing the nitty-gritty of the field.
